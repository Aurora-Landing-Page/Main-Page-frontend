Registration

What is "Aurora" all about?

Aurora is our annual flagship college fest, a vibrant celebration of creativity, talent, and innovation. It brings together students, artists, and enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds to participate in a kaleidoscope of events, including competitions, workshops, performances, and much more.

When and where will Aurora be held?

Aurora is scheduled to take place from 16-18 Feb at ABV-IIITM Gwalior. Our campus transforms into a dynamic hub during this time, welcoming participants and guests to partake in the festival's offerings.

How can I participate in Aurora?

Participation in Aurora is open to all college students. You can register for various events through our official website. Check out the event details and registration process on our website.

What to do after completing registration

After completing the registration for the event you are interested in, you must proceed to pay the required fee for the event. The payment must be done online, via payment option given on Registration or from dashboard

What types of events and competitions can I expect at Aurora?

Aurora hosts a diverse range of events, including cultural performances, competitive showcases, technical competitions, art exhibitions, seminars, and fun-filled games. From dance-offs to coding challenges, there's something for everyone!

How can I stay updated with the latest Aurora news and announcements?

To stay informed about all things Aurora, follow our official social media handles and regularly visit our website. We'll keep you updated on event schedules, guest announcements, registration details, and more.

How can I be part of organizing team of Aurora'24 and what are its benefits?

You can become a college ambassador for your college, and you will become the primary point of contact between your college and the Aurora'24 team.

Accommodation

What about the security facilities?

IIITM Gwalior campus has a vigilant and round-the-clock security service. There will be additional security to avoid thefts and other mishaps. Although Aurora team will not be responsible for any mishaps. Guests are requested to take care of their luggage and valuable items.

Documents that I should carry?

  1. Any valid Govt photo ID
  2. Print out of Email confirmation
  3. Gate pass
  4. Valid College ID for participants

Shall I carry my ID card with me?

It is mandatory for all guest participants to carry college IDs. This is for your own convenience. Moreover, you will be asked to produce your college ID cards at the time of allotment of rooms.

Payments and Charges

What is the payment procedure?

The payment procedure will be online. You can check the following link to register and avail accommodation: https://aurorafest.in

How do I know if my payment is confirmed?

You will receive an email from us confirming your registration and payment immediately.

Campus Facilities

How can I reach ABV-IIITM Gwalior Campus?

ABV-IIITM Gwalior is located in Hazira, Gwalior, around 6 kms from Gwalior Railway Station and 9 kms from Gwalior Airport. The campus is well connected by roads to all important places in Gwalior and one can easily reach the campus by taxis and auto rickshaws.

Are there any Hospital facilities in Campus?

Yes, ABV-IIITM Campus has a separate Hospital wing and an in-campus Ambulance fully equipped for any medical emergency.

What kind of food facilities are available?

Guest can purchase their meals from the cafeteria, food stalls, night cafeteria or hostel messes at subsidized rates. You can also order food online.

Will there be food stalls and amenities available at the venue?

Yes, Aurora ensures a delightful experience for all attendees by providing a variety of food stalls offering delicious cuisines. Additionally, amenities such as rest areas, first aid stations, and information desks will be available throughout the venue.

Can I enter Campus anytime?

No, the entry timings of Campus are 6 am to 10.30 pm.

Pronites and Concerts

Are there any celebrity guests or special performances lined up for Aurora?

Yes, Aurora aims to make the experience unforgettable by featuring renowned artists, industry experts, and captivating performances. Stay tuned for announcements on our website and social media channels regarding special guests and performances.

When and where will the passes for the Concerts be distributed?

After online payment, unique QR code will be mailed, which will be used for entry for the pronites. Wrist-bands will be provided at the entry to the campus.

What things are not allowed in the Pronites?

The following items are not allowed in pronites:
  1. Weapons and firearms
  2. Alcoholic beverages, cigarettes, or illegal substances
  3. Outside food and beverages
  4. Distracting items such as noisemakers, lasers, or boards
